Build Confidence via Meaningful Relationships.

What convinces a prospective student that a university is right for them? Academics? Of course. The campus? Probably. Actually, one of the most influencing factors that helps students decide on their college option is the reassurance that they will not be alone; that they will find people just like them with whom they can build meaningful relationships.

Mentor Match aims to do just that. It is a software that allows universities to pair prospective students (mentees) with current students (mentors) based on shared characteristics. These shared characteristics could be anything; hobbies, majors, nationalities. Mentor Match gives you the possibility to determine the matching criteria.

Step 1:
Construct your Match Form

Build a Match Forms that consist of questions you'd like to ask mentors and mentees.

Step 2:
Distribute your Match Form

Send the links to mentors and mentees to have them fill out the Match Form.

Step 3:
Make matches

Pair mentees with mentors based on the shared responses provided in the match forms.
